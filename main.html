About us

What is GRID?

GRID stands for Group for Resonance, Innovation, and Development, a student-driven initiative that brings together enthusiasts to work on cutting-edge projects, share ideas, and gain real-world technical skills.

Resonance: Collaborate with like-minded peers and work on exciting tech projects.

Innovation: Experiment with emerging technologies, participate in hackathons, and drive boundary-pushing projects.

Development: Gain hands-on experience through workshops, training, and mentorship, all while building an impressive portfolio.
